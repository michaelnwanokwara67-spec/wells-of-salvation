The Power of Prayer

Posted on February 15, 2026

Prayer is not just a religious ritual; it's a conversation with the Creator of the universe. Through prayer, we align our hearts with God's will and release His power into our lives and circumstances.

Jesus modeled a life of prayer. He often withdrew to lonely places to pray (Luke 5:16). If the Son of God needed communion with the Father, how much more do we?

Effective prayer is:

  • Persistent: Like the widow who kept bothering the judge (Luke 18:1-8).
  • Faith-filled: Believing that God hears and answers (Mark 11:24).
  • In line with God's will: Asking according to His purposes (1 John 5:14).

Don't be discouraged if answers don't come immediately. God's timing is perfect. Keep praying, keep trusting, and watch Him work.
