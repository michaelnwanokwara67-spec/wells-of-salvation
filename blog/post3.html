Living by Faith

Posted on April 2, 2026

Faith is not a feeling. It is a daily decision to trust God with what we cannot see and to move forward in obedience even when the path is uncertain.

Hebrews 11 describes faith as the assurance of things hoped for and the conviction of things not seen. When we live by faith, we acknowledge that God is at work behind the scenes, even when circumstances seem confusing.

Practical ways to live by faith:

  • Let God's promises shape your thoughts.
  • Choose obedience over comfort.
  • Speak truth from Scripture instead of your doubts.
  • Remember past victories to build confidence for today.

By maintaining a relationship with Christ, faith becomes our foundation. It keeps us grounded when storms arise and helps us remain faithful in every season.
