Walking in the Spirit

Posted on April 4, 2026

The Christian life is meant to be lived in the power of the Holy Spirit. Walking in the Spirit means allowing Him to direct our choices, control our desires, and teach us truth.

Paul wrote in Galatians 5 that those who live by the Spirit will not gratify the desires of the flesh. This doesn't mean we will never struggle, but it does mean the Spirit gives us the power to overcome.

Steps to walk in the Spirit:

  • Pray for guidance and sensitivity to the Spirit.
  • Spend time in God's Word and confess sin quickly.
  • Practice spiritual disciplines like worship, prayer, and fellowship.
  • Choose love, patience, and kindness in everyday relationships.

As you walk in the Spirit, your life becomes a testimony to God's presence. His fruit will grow in you, and others will see Christ shining through your actions.
